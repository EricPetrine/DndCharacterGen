Elf

Ability Score increases: Your dexterity increases by 2.
Age: Lifespan of 750
Alignment: Most elves are prone to chaos alignment
Size: Elves 5 to 6ft. Medium
Speed: Your walking speed is 30ft
Darkvision: Superior vision in dark/dim conditions. In dim light, you can view 60ft as if it were bright. In darkness you cannot discern color.
Keen Senses: You have proficiency in perception checks
Fey ancestry: Advantages on saving throws against being charmed. Sleep spells do not effect you.
Trance Elves do not need to sleep. Rather they meditate for 4 hours a day.
Languages Common/Elvish
