Tiefling

Ability Score Increase: Your Intelligence increases by 1. Your Charisma increases by 2
Age: Roughly the same rate of age as humans.
Alignment: Usually Evil, inclined to chaotic.
Size: Same size and build as humans, Medium.
Speed Your walking speed is 30ft.
Darkvision: Superior vision in dark/dim conditions. In dim light, you can view 60ft as if it were bright. In darkness you cannot discern color.
Hellish Resistance: You have resistance to fire damage.
Infernal Legacy You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip. At level 3 you can cast Hellish Rebuke as a 2nd level spell, you regain this ability after a long rest. At level 5, you can cast darkness once per long rest. Charisma is you spellcasting ability for these spells.
Languages: Common/Infernal
