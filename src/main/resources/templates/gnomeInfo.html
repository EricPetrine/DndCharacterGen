Gnome

Ability score increases: Your intelligence score increases by 2
Age: Lifespan of 350-500 years
Alignment: Often good and leaning toward lawful
Size: Between 3 to 4ft tall and about 40lbs, Small
Darkvision: Superior vision in dark/dim conditions. In dim light, you can view 60ft as if it were bright. In darkness you cannot discern color.
Gnome Cunning: You have advantage on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic.
Languages Common/Gnomish
