Dwarf

Ability Score increase: Your constitution increases by 2.
Age: Lifespan of 350 years
Alignment Most are lawful
Size: Between 4 and 5 feet tall and average 150lbs, Medium.
Speed Walking speed is 25ft, you are not reduced by heavy armor.
Darkvision: Superior vision in darkness or dim light. In dim light you can see within 60ft as if it were bright.
Dwarven Resilience: You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and resistance to poison damage.
Dwarven combat training: You have orificiency with battleaxes, handaxes, light hammers, and war hammers.
Tool proficiency: Proficiency with artisan's tools of choice (Smithing, brewing, or masonry).
Stonecunning: Whenever you make an intelligence check related to the origin of stonework, add double your proficiency bonus.
Languages: Common/Dwarvish
