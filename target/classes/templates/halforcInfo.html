Half-Orc

Ability score increases: Your Strength increases by 2 and your Constitution increases by 1.
Alignment: Typically chaotic of any type. Size: Typically 5 to 6ft tall, Medium
Speed Your walking speed is 30ft.
Darkvision: Superior vision in dark/dim conditions. In dim light, you can view 60ft as if it were bright. In darkness you cannot discern color.
Menacing You have proficiency in Intimidation.
Relentless Endurance: When reduced to 0hp, you can regain 1hp. This ability can only be used once per long rest
Savage Attacks: When you score a critical hit with a melee weapon, you roll one extra damage dice
Languages: Common/Orc
